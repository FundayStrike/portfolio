About Me

My name is Bryan, a Junior College Year 2 student studying at Anderson Serangoon Junior College, taking the subject combination of H2 Physics, H2 Computing, H2 Mathematics and H2 Economics. My main passion lies in coding, and I have dabbled in various computing domains such as competitive programming, machine learning and cybersecurity. Over the course of my computing journey, I took part in numerous hackathons and competitions. Here are some of my notable participations:

  • Attained 8th place in Cyberthon 2024
  • Made four pull requests during Hacktoberfest 2023
  • Attained Bronze at the Singapore Mathematical Olympiad 2024 (Open Section)

In addition, I have also immersed myself in a few volunteering projects such as the student-initiated O-Levels Computing Booster Programme to teach Secondary 4 O-Level Computing students and I was also part of my school's ICT Champion programme to educate fellow schoolmates about current technologies.
